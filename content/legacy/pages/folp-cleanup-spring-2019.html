THANK YOU AGAIN! …

To All Who Participated in the Spring 2019 Lafayette Park Clean-Up Day on April 13, 2019.

Neighbors, Friends of all ages turned out on a beautiful spring day to do light clean-up work in the park — leaf raking, weed pulling, breaking up fallen tree branches, sweeping,  and many other chores that make the park all that much more special.  These efforts fill gaps they city does not do… The day was a huge success due to all who participated!  Thank you to all!

 The FOLP would like to thank Frank Van Riper for taking these wonderful photographs.
