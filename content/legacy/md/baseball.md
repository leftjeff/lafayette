The Capitol City Little League  

Capitol City Little League is a community-based 501(c)(3) organization that is incorporated in the District of Columbia and run by parents and other volunteers. Part of the national Little League (LL) organization, www.littleleague.org, Capitol City LL has been providing baseball and softball opportunities for the pre-teen youth of NW Washington for over 20 years.

![Capitol City Little League](http://thefolp.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/cap_league.jpg)

The league is administered by a Board of Directors (11) from which officers (4) are elected. There are six divisions (4 baseball and 2 softball) which are run by commissioners (6). Capitol City LL serves boys and girls who range in age from 5 to 14 years old.
