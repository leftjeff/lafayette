Volunteer Park Clean-up

Friends of Lafayette Park would like to thank all who participated in our Spring 2011 Cleanup. Your efforts are greatly appreciated. It was a wonderful opportunity to enjoy the beautiful spring weather and meet your neighbors. We would also like to thank Frank Van Riper and Judith Goodman for taking photographs. Also, special thanks to Lobster Truck DC for stopping by and letting us have a taste of Maine and Broadbranch Market for providing coffee and doughnuts.
